Return %
Each point on the graph shows your return on the total amount invested up to that date, including returns from mutual fund units you had already sold before that date.
The app adds two things at each date: the value of units you still held, plus cash received from units sold before that date. It then compares that total with all buys made up to that date. This is why sold units still count in the return graph.

Timing of your investments
This shows the fund price (NAV) on the days you bought or sold. If you bought when the price was lower, that investment usually has a better return. If you bought just before the price fell, that investment may look weaker for a while. Use this to understand how well you timed your investments in the past!
